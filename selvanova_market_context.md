## 2. Best-fit guest personas

### Persona A — Family with children, rental car or rideshare budget

This is probably the strongest natural persona. They care about space, sleeping separation, kitchen, laundry, pool, parking, security, and predictable check-in. They are less likely to need a nightlife-dense location and more likely to value a calm base after beach or park days.

**Why they book:** 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, terrace, pools, gym, parking, security, and kitchen reduce vacation stress.  
**Why revenue improves:** families often book longer stays and evaluate total value per person, not just nightly ADR.  
**Why Selvanova-specific:** Selvanova’s controlled-access residential feel and common amenities match family anxieties better than a noisy downtown studio.

Impact: **conversion high, ADR medium-high, occupancy high, review quality high, ranking medium, difficulty low, confidence high.**

### Persona B — Friend group / multi-couple leisure stay

This group wants private bedrooms, common space, TV/living area, pool, access to Quinta Avenida by car, and a lower per-person cost than multiple hotel rooms.

**Why they book:** “3BR + shared social space + pools + 10 min by car to Quinta/beach” is easy to understand.  
**Why revenue improves:** the host can charge more total revenue than a 1–2BR unit while still looking affordable per guest.  
**Why Selvanova-specific:** inland location is less of a problem when the group is splitting rideshare/car costs.

Impact: **conversion high, ADR high, occupancy medium, review quality medium, ranking medium, difficulty low, confidence high.**

### Persona C — Longer-stay remote/hybrid worker or relocating guest

They need Wi‑Fi, AC, desk/work surface, kitchen, laundry, parking, quiet, and grocery access. Public Selvanova comps explicitly mention long stays and working from home.

**Why they book:** the listing feels livable, not just photogenic.  
**Why revenue improves:** longer stays fill shoulder-season gaps and reduce turnover cost.  
**Why Selvanova-specific:** residential calm, stores, parking, and larger floor plans are more valuable than immediate beach access for month-long stays.

Impact: **conversion medium-high, ADR medium, occupancy high, review quality high, ranking medium, difficulty medium, confidence medium-high.**

### Persona D — Activity-based Riviera Maya traveler

These guests plan Xcaret/Xplor, cenotes, Tulum, Cozumel ferry, snorkeling/diving, and town nights. They need a comfortable launchpad.

**Why they book:** the apartment makes the trip logistically easier.  
**Why revenue improves:** activity-based itineraries often require several nights and value reliable parking/check-in.  
**Why Selvanova-specific:** it sits as a practical inland base rather than a single-purpose beach stay.

Impact: **conversion medium, ADR medium, occupancy medium-high, review quality medium, ranking low-medium, difficulty low, confidence medium.**

> “You are not beachfront, but you get much more space, calmer surroundings, controlled access, parking, pools, a real kitchen/laundry setup, and easy car access to beach, Quinta, parks, cenotes, and shopping.”

That clarity improves click-through, conversion, review quality, and ranking.
