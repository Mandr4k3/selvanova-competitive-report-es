## Seasonal Recommendations

Peak protection:

- December-February and late March/holiday periods: protect peak dates with 3-5 night minimums, avoid early discounting, and defend premium only after trust proof improves.
- July-August: use a moderate summer premium, but watch booked-listing pace because future-booked supply declines after early summer.

Shoulder conversion:

- April-June and November: use flexible rules and weekly-stay messaging. Preserve some weekend premium but avoid overpricing midweek.
- Use 52-day lead time as the 3BR decision window: if the next 52 days are under-booked, tune pricing before the market has passed the natural booking window.

Low-season defense:

- September is the worst month and should be treated as an occupancy and review-building month.
- Use 2-night minimums for gaps and midweek, visible weekly discounts, flexible/moderate cancellation, and stronger value copy.
- Do not try to defend top-performer ADR in September without dashboard evidence that the listing is already converting.
